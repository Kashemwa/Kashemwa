### About Me

I'm a **GIS & Web GIS Developer** specialized in **Geographic Information Systems (GIS)**, **spatial data pipelines**, **GeoAI**, and **cloud architecture**.

I design and ship end-to-end geospatial solutions — from **NestJS / Node.js** spatial APIs and **PostGIS** databases to **Leaflet**, **OpenLayers**, **CesiumJS** map clients, **GeoServer** OGC services, and **AWS** deployments.

I train **GIS AI models** for **object detection** and **semantic segmentation** on geospatial imagery — building datasets from raster/vector sources, fine-tuning models with **PyTorch** and **TensorFlow**, and deploying inference pipelines for conservation and land-use analysis.

I contribute to technical documentation on the **[Virunga Foundation documentation platform](https://docs.virunga.org)** — publishing guides, architecture notes, and operational references for geospatial systems and conservation technology workflows.
